Welcome to the Weather App! This app allows you to check the weather forecast for any city worldwide.

Developed by Samson Mulugeta, Zoe Cheng, and Kyle Doung, this app utilizes the OpenWeatherMap API to fetch real-time weather data.

Feel free to explore different cities and check their current weather conditions as well as a 5-day forecast.

For any documentation help, feel free to check out our documentation tab.
